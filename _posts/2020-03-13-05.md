The very basic definition of poly-time computable function.
<!--more-->

A function $f:\bits^* \to \bits^* $ is polynomial-time computable if there exists a polynomial-time algorithm $F$ mapping $x$ to $f(x)$, $\forall x \in \bits^*$.
